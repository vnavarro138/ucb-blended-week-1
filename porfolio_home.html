About Me



Monica Bumb was born and raised in San Jose, California with four siblings. Their fondest memories are from playing at the Piazza house in the backyard. She studiued Business at California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo. Two weeks before graduation, Monica got a job at a semiconductor company in Sunnyvale as a Financial Analyst. After dabbling in accounting and finance for a couple of years, Monica soon stumbled upon Excel spreadsheets and the transportation industry. Monica continues to grow her passion for learning and coding at DCL Logistics as a Manager of Transportation and Logistics. She is taking a full-stack Development class at UC Berekely to continue her growth in the programming field.

In her free time, Monica likes to hang out with her fiance, Mark Hayden. They love to hike, play video games and board games, play with their calico cat Kit-9, and walk Pup Pup. There favorite things to do is run away on the weekend and listen to the San Francisco Symphony.
